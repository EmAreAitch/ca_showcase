Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide a domain name?

No — I guide you through purchasing your domain so you fully own it and control DNS settings.

Will my website be editable without coding?

Yes — I provide a user-friendly CMS and a short handover so you can update text, images, and service pages yourself.

Do you handle data privacy and security?

Yes — I implement privacy-conscious forms, SSL encryption, and recommend data-retention and security practices aligned with the Indian IT Act, the SPDI rules, and the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023 where applicable. For legal assurance, consult your compliance advisor or legal counsel.

How much do you charge for website maintenance?

I charge ₹300/hour for website maintenance tasks (non-urgent). Urgent same-day updates are charged at ₹500/hour. There are no monthly lock-ins; you pay only for the time spent on your updates. For ongoing retained support, we can agree on a very affordable monthly package separately.

Can I update my plan or request new features later?

Yes — you can request additional features or upgrades at any time, and I’ll provide a custom quote based on your needs.

How long does it take to build a website?

Typically, a project takes between 3 to 6 weeks depending on complexity, content readiness, and review cycles.

Will I work directly with you?

Yes — you’ll be communicating directly with me throughout the project, ensuring clear and personal service.

What’s included in your free website audit?

I review your site’s design, structure, mobile responsiveness, SEO basics, and loading speed — providing actionable recommendations for improvement.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes — most clients pay 50% upfront and 50% on completion before launch. Larger projects can be split into more milestones if needed.

What if I don’t have branding or a logo yet?

No problem — I can recommend affordable designers or provide a basic logo design add-on so your site looks professional from day one.

Do you write the website copy?

I can either use the content you provide or offer a copywriting add-on to make your messaging clear, professional, and client-focused.

Will my site be optimised for Google?

Yes — I set up your site with SEO-friendly structure, meta tags, alt text, and page speed best practices. Ongoing SEO campaigns are available as a separate service.

Can you integrate third-party tools?

Yes — I can connect CRMs, booking systems, payment gateways (UPI, cards), and other business tools so they work seamlessly with your website.

What happens after launch?

You get full ownership of your site and all files, plus a short training session on using your CMS. I’m available for ongoing updates and improvements as needed.
